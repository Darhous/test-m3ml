# Feature: Tenant Provisioning

**Module:** Tenant and Organization Management (Platform Kernel). Covers
onboarding a new tenant (customer organization) — creating its Keycloak
Organization (Module 1), its `tenant_id` partition (this Module's
`multi-tenancy-isolation` Feature), and its initial admin user, as one
coordinated workflow. Cross-Feature Dependency: reuses two already-
researched Engines rather than introducing a new one.

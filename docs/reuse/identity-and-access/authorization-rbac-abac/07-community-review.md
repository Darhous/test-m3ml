# Community Review — Authorization (RBAC/ABAC)

| Candidate | Governance | Adoption Signal |
|---|---|---|
| OPA | **CNCF Graduated** (highest maturity tier; peers at this tier include Kubernetes, Prometheus) | Extremely broad adoption across cloud-native/enterprise policy enforcement, well beyond a single vendor's ecosystem |
| Casbin | Community-led, no single foundation | Very broad language-ecosystem coverage (a differentiator, not a governance weakness) — widely embedded in many frameworks |
| Keycloak Authorization Services | Same as `../authentication/` | Real but narrower adoption specifically for this sub-feature compared to OPA's broad policy-engine adoption |

OPA's CNCF Graduated status is the single strongest long-term-
sustainability signal found anywhere in this Module's research so far —
stronger than Keycloak's own Incubating status.

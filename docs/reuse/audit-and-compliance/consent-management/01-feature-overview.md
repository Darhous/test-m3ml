# Feature: Consent Management

**Module:** Audit and Compliance (Platform Kernel)
**Source (Discovery):** Constitution Section 22 (Consent — Accepted);
Wave 3 Capability "Consent" (Governance category, Evidenced). Covers
tracking and enforcing **clinical/patient data consent** (a patient
consenting to their result being shared with a referring doctor, to
AI-assisted processing per Section 28, or to research use) — distinct
from website/marketing cookie consent, which this research explicitly
does not conflate with clinical consent (see `02-global-search.md`).

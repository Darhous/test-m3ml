## Landscape Notes

Three credible open-source GRC (Governance, Risk, Compliance) platforms
found: **CISO Assistant** (policy/control management, internal audits,
risk assessments, compliance packages including ISO 27001/GDPR,
dashboards), **GovReady-Q** (self-service compliance assessments and
documentation, security-assessment-automation focus), **Eramba** (mature,
long-standing, infosec-team-oriented policy/risk/compliance management).
All 3 are general-purpose GRC platforms (built for IT/security compliance
programs, e.g., SOC 2/ISO 27001), not healthcare-specific — a real,
honestly-reported gap: none is purpose-built for lab/healthcare
regulatory tracking (Egypt's ETA/PDPL/EDA/UHI findings, Wave 11).

# Feature: Compliance Tracking

**Module:** Audit and Compliance (Platform Kernel)
**Source (Discovery):** Wave 3 Capability "Compliance" (Governance,
Inferred, Egypt-specific detail Wave 11); Constitution Section 58 (Risk
Governance, Accepted). Covers tracking the platform's own regulatory
compliance posture (Egypt's 4 researched regulations, Wave 11; future
markets' requirements as they're researched) as an internal
governance/risk-management capability — distinct from `policy-engine`
(runtime authorization decisions) and `immutable-audit-trail` (event
storage).

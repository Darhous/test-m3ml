**Final Decision: BUILD (per-gateway adapters) + REFERENCE (Omnipay
architecture pattern)**

No stack-neutral open-source product exists for this Feature; Fawry and
Paymob are commercial Vendor decisions, not OSS to adopt. Consistent
with Module 4's `device-protocol-adapters` precedent: BUILD is the
correct, evidence-based answer when a Feature is inherently
per-provider by definition.

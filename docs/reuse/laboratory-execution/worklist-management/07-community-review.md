**SENAITE**: 20+ year lineage (Bika LIMS, 2002 origin), active add-on
ecosystem, multiple commercial consultancies providing support (NARALabs
and others), no single-vendor risk in the open-core sense, but also no
foundation governance (not CNCF/ASF/Eclipse-hosted).

**OpenELIS Global**: Backed by the Open ELIS Foundation and University of
Iowa Hygienic Laboratory IT staff, used at national scale across multiple
public-health systems (per DIGI-UW/Open ELIS Foundation documentation) —
stronger institutional backing than SENAITE's consultancy model, though
also not foundation-governed in the CNCF/ASF sense.

Neither is rejected on community grounds — both pass the program's
abandonment/dead-project screen comfortably.

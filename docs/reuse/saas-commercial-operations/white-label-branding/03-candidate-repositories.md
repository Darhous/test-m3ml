No new candidate. No single "white-label branding engine" product
exists or is needed — the platform's own frontend (BUILD, by
definition, since it is the platform's UI) applies a per-tenant theme
configuration (colors, logo, custom domain), while already-adopted
Engines with their own theming layers (Keycloak, Superset, Documenso,
Alfresco) apply their own theme configuration consistently.

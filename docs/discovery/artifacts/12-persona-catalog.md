# Persona Catalog (Discovery, Phase 12)

**Status: Draft — Assumption-Driven Autonomous Run.** Synthesized from
Phase 02 stakeholder needs and Phase 03 Actor inventory — no new content
invented beyond reformatting already-logged Inferred facts into persona
shape. Only the 4 highest-leverage personas are detailed (per Phase 07's
finding that Patients, Doctors, Laboratory Staff, and Laboratory Management
are the most load-bearing categories); the remaining 11 stakeholder
categories retain their Phase 02 table entries in `.claude/context/
stakeholders.md` rather than being force-fit into full personas without
better evidence.

## Patient

- **Goals (Inferred):** get a trustworthy result quickly, understand what
  it means, pay a fair and clear amount.
- **Frustrations (Inferred):** unclear specimen-rejection reasons, opaque
  billing, inconvenient collection logistics.
- **Touches Value Streams:** VS1, VS2, VS3, VS4.
- **Confidence:** Medium — grounded in Phase 02's stakeholder table, not a
  real patient interview.

## Referring Doctor

- **Goals (Inferred):** fast and reliable result delivery, ability to order
  on a patient's behalf, a trustworthy verification process.
- **Frustrations (Inferred):** delayed results, unclear specimen-rejection
  escalation (Phase 07, Open Question #20), no visibility into result
  turnaround.
- **Touches Value Streams:** VS1, VS2.
- **Confidence:** Medium.

## Laboratory Staff (Result Verifier sub-role, per Phase 07)

- **Goals (Inferred):** clear worklists, accurate specimen tracking, an
  unambiguous authority gate for verification (Phase 07's Result Verifier
  Role).
- **Frustrations (Inferred):** unclear verification eligibility criteria
  for different test types (Phase 07, Open Question #19 — the single
  highest-priority Open Question in this entire Discovery run given its
  clinical-safety weight).
- **Touches Value Streams:** VS1, VS2, VS3.
- **Confidence:** Medium — the *role* is well-evidenced (Phase 07); the
  *person's* actual frustrations are Inferred, not observed.

## Laboratory Management

- **Goals (Inferred):** operational visibility (throughput, rejection
  rate), staff workload balancing, defensible compliance posture.
- **Frustrations (Inferred):** rejection-rate visibility gaps (ties to
  Phase 07 Open Question #20, escalation threshold).
- **Touches Value Streams:** VS1, VS2, VS3.
- **Confidence:** Medium.

## Remaining 11 Stakeholder Categories

Not expanded into full personas here — see `.claude/context/

Inferred primary needs at the same confidence level. Expanding them further
